Belong to the Republic of Vanuatu

Message from the Chairman

"Welcome to the Citizenship Office and Commission of the Republic of Vanuatu — a Constitutional body entrusted with the integrity of Vanuatu citizenship in every form."

To our fellow ni-Vanuatu, and to those abroad who hope to make these islands their home, I extend a sincere welcome. Our charge is simple to state and demanding to keep: to administer every pathway to citizenship — by birth, descent, naturalisation, marriage and investment — fairly, openly, and with an integrity that does not bend.

Charlie Amalmiye'rr ManielChairman · Citizenship Office & Commission
A national citizenship authority

More than an investment programme

We are the Constitutional authority for every Vanuatu citizenship — not only the investment routes the world knows us for, but citizenship by birth, descent, naturalisation and marriage. That mandate is written into the Citizenship Act [Cap 112] and Chapter 3 of the national Constitution.

Integrity at the core

Due diligence is our foundation

The worth of a Vanuatu passport depends on who carries it. That is why every application is examined — independently, and in layers — before the Commission decides.

Licensed agents only

No direct applications. Every file is lodged by a vetted, authorised agent.

Independent FIU screening

The Vanuatu Financial Intelligence Unit conducts independent background checks.

International verification

Accredited third-party firms verify identity and history across jurisdictions.

Governance & accountability

An accountable, Constitutional institution

The Commission is a Constitutional body, governed by the Citizenship Act [Cap 112] and the national Constitution. Its powers, its process and its people are defined by law — and answerable to it.
