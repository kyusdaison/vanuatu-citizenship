Fraud awareness

Official Vanuatu Government citizenship sites always end in .gov.vu. The Office never collects payments through unofficial channels. Deal only with licensed agents on the list above, verify identities, and report anything suspicious.

Become an authorised agent

Agents are licensed by the Citizenship Commission and must adhere to government-prescribed pricing and due-diligence requirements. To enquire about licensing, contact the Secretary General's office.
