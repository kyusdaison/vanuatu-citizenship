Applying for citizenship?

Applications can only be lodged through agents licensed by the Citizenship Commission — direct applications are not accepted for the investment programmes. Start by choosing an authorised agent.

Office access (PMO compound)

The office sits within the Prime Minister's Office compound. Visitors require a prior appointment, must dress appropriately, and must observe the compound's security and access rules.
